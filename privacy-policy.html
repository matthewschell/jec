Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 8, 2026

At Johnston Electrical & Contracting ("we," "us," or "our"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you use our website or request our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us to facilitate quotes, sales, and services. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, and service address.
  • Project Information: Photos of your property, electrical panels, or renovation sites that you upload or send to us.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following business purposes:

  • To provide accurate estimates and quotes for electrical and contracting work.
  • To communicate with you regarding your project, appointments, or inquiries.
  • To schedule and complete installation, repair, or renovation services.
  • To process invoices and payments.

3. Sharing of Information

We respect your data. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

However, we may share your information with trusted third parties solely to facilitate the services you have requested:

  • Subcontractors & Partners: We may share project details (such as site address and photos) with specialized subcontractors (e.g., excavators, drywallers, or specialized trades) strictly to complete your project.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.
