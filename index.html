Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our Electrical & Construction services.

Are you fully licenced and insured?
Yes. We are a fully licenced electrical contractor (ECRA/ESA) and carry liability insurance and WSIB coverage. We are also proud to be Baeumler Approved, which requires strict vetting of our insurance and professional history.
Do you provide services in Lindsay and Fenelon Falls?
Yes. While we are based in Woodville, we proudly serve the entire Kawartha Lakes region, including Lindsay, Little Britain, Cannington, Beaverton, Fenelon Falls, and Manilla.
Do you handle permits for construction projects?
Absolutely. Whether we are building a deck, finishing a basement, or upgrading your electrical panel, we handle the entire permit process with the municipality and the Electrical Safety Authority (ESA) to ensure your project is 100% compliant.
Do I need to upgrade my electrical panel to 200 Amp?
If you are adding a hot tub, EV charger, or finishing a basement, you may need more power. We can assess your current 60 or 100 Amp service and recommend a safe, future-proof upgrade if necessary.
Do you provide free estimates?
Yes! We provide transparent, detailed quotes for all projects. Simply click the "Get a Quote" button below or give us a call to schedule a site visit.
