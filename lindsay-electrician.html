How much does a panel upgrade cost in Lindsay?
Every home is different, so panel upgrade costs vary based on your home's age, current service size, location of the panel, and distance from the street. We provide free, detailed quotes with no obligation. Call us at 705-344-7770 to schedule an assessment.
Who handles the permits with the City of Kawartha Lakes?
We do. For electrical, we file with the ESA. For structural renovations (like decks or additions), we handle the building permits at the City of Kawartha Lakes Service Centre on Colborne St.
