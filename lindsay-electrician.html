Common Electrical Projects in Lindsay Homes

Historic Homes (Pre-1960)

Many homes in Lindsay's North Ward and downtown core still have original knob-and-tube wiring. We can assess your home's wiring and recommend the best approach for safe, insurance-compliant upgrades while preserving your home's character. We work directly with insurance providers to ensure all safety standards are met.

Panel Upgrades (60A → 200A)

If you live in an older Lindsay home and want to add central A/C, a hot tub, or an EV charger, your 60 or 100 Amp service won't cut it. We handle the entire upgrade process including Hydro One coordination and ESA inspections. Most upgrades are completed in 1-2 days.

Rural & Cottage Properties

Properties along Sturgeon Lake, Lake Scugog, or in rural Kawartha Lakes often need generator hookups (GenerLink), well pump upgrades, or underground service lines. We're equipped to handle rural electrical work and understand the unique challenges of cottage country.
